What is Smash Flash Knockout?!

Smash Flash Knockout is the competition being run by GameProxy that is centered around the hugely popular game Super Smash Flash 2. Smash Flash is a clone of Nintendo's paid game Smash Bros that anyone can play for free on GameProxy. The game is proudly developed by McLeodGaming Inc. and GameProxy owes huge amounts of thanks to the developers of Smash Flash.

If you haven't played Smash Flash yet, you're missing out! Just play the game on GameProxy now!

Great, how do I enter?

If your name's on the list, just pop along to the FIT room at 1:10 PM (just after lunch) on Friday 17th May. We'll show you what to do.

If you would like to spectate, just bring £1 to enter. You won't be able to play, but you'll be able to watch others play via the big screen where we'll show screencasts.

Please note that people outside of Norwich School cannot enter, and on-site security is in place to prevent this.

Where does the money go?

100% of the profits that are made on the day will be going to charities ─ specifically Step into Tech and Make a Wish Foundation. You are of course more than welcome to donate more than the required amount!

But what would I get if I win?!

I knew you'd ask that question! That's why we've got some pretty nice prizes lined up: lots of Cadburys chocolate, some cool 3D-printed objects and some vouchers for GameProxy Pro!

Don't worry if you do not win ─ participants who don't will be getting a free 1 month GameProxy Pro voucher on us. There's no reason not to play!
